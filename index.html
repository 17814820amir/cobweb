About Bhutan

Bhutan is small land locked country sandwitch betweeen India and China. It is also known as the land of the Thunder dragon. It has the population of over 7 lhaks where it has more than 70% forest coverage. 90% people in the country follow buddhism as the major religion where less than 10% in country follows other religion. The country has been ruled by the monarch from 1907 till the date. Most the country are being hit hard by the covid, where the economy were being crippled and many became jobless. However in Bhutan, under the dynamic leadership of his majesty the 5th king, the country had gain victory in battle with covid-19. Overmoever his majesty the king, granted kidu to the people who had been hit hard by the covid. mainly for the people who jobless during the closure of the tourism sector. however everything kept under the control due to imense hard from his majesty and the prime minister of Bhutan.
